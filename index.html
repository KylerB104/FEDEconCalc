How To Use This Tool

  1. Pick a region. The app only fills that region's 1956 GDP base. It does not fill the budget for you.
  2. Enter old numbers. Put the pre-budget tax revenue, tax rates, and department spending in the Old columns.
  3. Enter new numbers. Put the proposed budget's tax revenue, tax rates, and department spending in the New columns.
  4. Add real-world context. Use the sliders for business climate, labor protection, housing pressure, and infrastructure stress.
  5. Fill the narrative fields. Add hardest-hit metro, strongest metro, hardest-hit sector, strongest sector, and infrastructure pressure.
  6. Read the output. The snapshot, budget table, and charts update automatically. Use Copy or Export Snapshot when finished.

Use Example Fill only when you want to see the Sierra sample format. It is a demo, not a default.
